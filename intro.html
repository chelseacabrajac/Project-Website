Chelsea Cabrajac
Chelsea is multicultural and multifaceted in design, with a passion for travel and culinary experiences. After traveling the world with sports and an extremely decorated athletic journey, Chelsea has built a career around solving complex problems under pressure.

This journey began as a highly decorated dual-sport NCAA Division I Athlete, instilling a strong work ethic and exceptional ability to perform in high-stress environments. That foundation led her to a career as a professional firefighter with the Long Beach Fire Department, serving on some of the nation's busiest resources and consistently demonstrating unwavering attention to detail in critical situations while protecting life, property, and the environment.

This passion for impactful problem-solving is now channeled into the world of data science. Having completed the TripleTen Data Science program, her proficiency exists in Python, SQL, Data Visualization, and Machine Learning. These skills are used to analyze complex datasets, uncover crucial insights, and deliver data-driven results that directly improve business decision-making.

Chelsea has a relentless intention to contribute to organizations, seeking as a uniquely resilient and results-oriented data professional. Success is found in environments where a diverse background can offer a fresh perspective and drive significant impact.
